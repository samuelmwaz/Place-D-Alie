About
Place D'Alie

Located in PHI, Lusaka, next to Bennie Mwiinga Police Post, Place D'Alie is a casual dining restaurant where community and flavour meet. We serve freshly made meals with love — from crispy chips to wood-fired pizzas — in a warm, welcoming space built for everyone.
